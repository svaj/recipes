
2.5 cps flour
1.5 cp sugar
1 cp butter
1 egg
1.5 tsp baking powder
1 tsp vanilla
0.5 tsp salt
------------------------------
0.5 almod extract
5 drops of red food cloring
1 square of unsweetened chocolate melted
0.5 cp chopped walknuts

mix at low speed: mix first 7 ingredients until blended well

beat it at 3 mins at medium speed

divide dough into 3rds
each should be 10 3/8th oz

in one: add almond and food colring to one,
in another: chocolate
3rd: get walnuts

loaf pan with straight side line with wax paper (sides too)

layer dough ontop of eachother

fridge for 4 hrs
take out of loaf, cut lengthwise down the middle

slice up each long bit into 1/4" cookie

bake at 350F for 10-12 mins
