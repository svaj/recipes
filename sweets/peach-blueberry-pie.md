Peach & blueberry pie recipe
----------

### Filling

* 3 cups peches
* 1 cup blueberries
* 2 TBs lemon juice
* 1 cup sugar
* 1/4 cup tapioca pearls
* 1/2 tsp salt

### When topping with additional crust:

* 2 TBs butter for a layer between filling and crust
* 1 egg yolk mixed with splash of cream to brush the crust


1. Preheat oven to 425F
2. you make a pastry crust with crisco/shortening pls -- DO NOT SUBSTITUTE BUTTER FOR SHORTENING OR RISK BURNING IT
3. sprinkle juice from peaches on all fruits
4. mix sugar with tapioca and salt, toss with fruits to macerate for 15m
5. pour into shell, plop butter over (but under the top layer of crust).  
6. egg and cream is to egg wash crust if you have that top layer
7. bake at 425F for 45m - 50m
